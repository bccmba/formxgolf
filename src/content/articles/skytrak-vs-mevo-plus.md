The SkyTrak+ and Mevo Plus are the two launch monitors most serious amateur golfers seriously consider when setting up a home simulator or practice setup. Both are in the $2,000–$2,500 range. Both are legitimately accurate. But they're built differently and optimized for different use cases.

Here's a direct comparison.

## Quick Verdict

- **Choose SkyTrak+** if you want the best simulator software ecosystem and plan to use it indoors primarily
- **Choose Mevo Plus** if you want portability and plan to use it on the driving range or outdoors

---

## Accuracy

Both units measure the key data points — ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, carry distance — with enough accuracy for meaningful feedback.

In independent testing, both units track within 2–5% of TrackMan (the tour-standard reference). For a home practice device, that's more than good enough.

Slight edge: **SkyTrak+** for iron shots indoors. **Mevo Plus** for driver outdoors.

---

## Software & Simulator Compatibility

This is where SkyTrak+ clearly wins.

SkyTrak+ integrates natively with **E6 Connect**, **The Golf Club 2019**, **WGT**, and others. The app experience is polished and the course library is excellent.

Mevo Plus works with **E6 Connect** and **FSX Play**, but the integration feels slightly less seamless. That said, Mevo Plus recently added Awesome Golf simulation, which has improved.

**Winner: SkyTrak+**

---

## Portability

Mevo Plus wins this convincingly. It's compact, runs on battery, and sets up in 60 seconds on a driving range mat. SkyTrak+ is designed for a fixed indoor setup.

If you want one device that works at home AND at the range: **Mevo Plus**.

## Bottom Line

For most people building a home simulator: **SkyTrak+**. The software ecosystem is better and it'll be the centerpiece of your setup.

If you travel, play outdoors, or want a device you'll take to the range: **Mevo Plus**.

Either way, you're getting a device that will genuinely improve your game.
